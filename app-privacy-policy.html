WOWidget app privacy policy

The app does not collect any user data.

Crash reports

If the app crashes while you're using it, anonymized data about the crash will be collected to help me identify the cause of the crash and hopefully fix it in a future update. These "crash logs" contain information such as the state of the app, operating system, and device at the time of the crash, but not your private data.

Crash reports do not include any user data such as URL.

This policy is effective as of 20 May 2020.
