The story of WOWidget

11th of May 2020

I needed to regularly check critical business information (sales, system health).
I used Safari to access our dashboards, but that always seemed like too many clicks, especially in the morning.
I'm actively using iPhone widgets and wanted to see our information in a widget.
I couldn't find an app to achieve this, so I made one.
WOWidget was born.
