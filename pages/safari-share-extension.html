WOWidget via Safari share extension

  1. Open website in Safari.
  2. Tap on share button.
  3. Tap on WOWidget icon.

If you don't see the WOWidget icon

  1. Tap on More.
  2. Tap on Edit and enable WOWidget.
  3. For easier use add WOWidget into Favorites
  4. Tap on Done.
